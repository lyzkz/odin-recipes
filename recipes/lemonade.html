Lemonade

The Best summer drink


Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • Sparkling water
  • maple syrup

Steps

  1. Cute the lemon into 3 piece. You should have one round slice and both sides
  2. Squeeze out the lemon juices from both ends of the lemon
  3. Add a sprinkle of maple syrup to a glass if you wish to use a sweetener
  4. Place 2 ice cubes then the slice of lemon into the glass
  5. Add freshly squeezed lemon juice to the glass
  6. Give it a quick stir so the maple syrup and lemon juice mixes
  7. Add sparkling water to the glass and top it of with ice and another slice of lemon if you wish
