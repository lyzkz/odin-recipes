Gyoza

Gyoza is a type of dumpling popular in japan. Traditionally, they are filled with minced pork, cabbage and mushroom and seasoned with garlic, ginger and soy sauce.


Ingredients

  • Minced pork
  • Cabbage
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • dumpling wrapper

Steps

  1. Cut the cabbage and mushroom into small pieces
  2. Add salt to the cut cabbage and let the cabbage dehydrate
  3. Mix minced pork with the seasoning
  4. Knead it until the meat feels slimy
  5. Squeeze the water out of the cabbage
  6. Add the mushroom and cabbage to the pork mixture and mix well
  7. Wrap the mixture into dumpling wrappers
  8. Heat a pan and drizzle some oil
  9. Pan fry the dumplings
