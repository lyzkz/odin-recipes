Salad

A simple and delicious salad


Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Corn
  • Lemon Juice
  • Sugar
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegetables
  2. Cut the vegetables into sized you prefer
  3. Add kale to a bowl and some olive oil
  4. Massage the kale until it get's softer
  5. Add the other vegetables to the bowl
  6. Add the seasoning and season it to taste with salt and pepper
